Video Games

Valorant

Valorant is a first person shooter set in the future. Each person on a team have special abilities called "heros". The game involves two teams of 5 playes on each team. Your objective on your team is to work together to beat the other team, like any other competetive game.

Apex Legends is a Battle Royal based game where multiple teams of three drop on a map to see who will be the last team standing. Each teams has pre-picked characters called "Legends" which have special abilities that help their team win the game. The role of each character varies but it is important to pick the characters that work well together in order to have a higher chance to succeed.
